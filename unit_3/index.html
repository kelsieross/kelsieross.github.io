About me!

Kelsie's cooking kraze is a place to create fast, easy recipes that are healthy! After being diagnosed with adrenal fatigue in 2017, I knew that changing my diet was going to be 75% of the battle. From that came my newfound love of making my favorite dishes healthy and focused on auto immune and adrenal health. I focus on three types of meals:

  • Quick weeknight grub
  • Healthy entertianing
  • Semi-sweet indulgence

About me!

Learning to create fast, easy recipes that are healthy! After being diagnosed with adrenal fatigue in 2017, I knew that changing my diet was going to be 75% of the battle. From that came my newfound love of making my favorite dishes healthy and focused on auto immune and adrenal health. I focus on quick weeknight grub, healthy entertaining and semi-sweet indulgences!
